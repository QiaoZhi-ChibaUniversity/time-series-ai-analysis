date: observation time

GPP: gross primary productivity

SW_IN_F: incoming shortwave radiation (gap-filled)

PPFD_IN_F_MDS: photosynthetic photon flux density (PPFD)

PPFD_IN_F_MDS_QC: quality flag for PPFD

PAR_BK: backup PAR (derived from shortwave radiation)

PAR: primary PAR variable

JAXAPAR: satellite-derived PAR (Himawari)

window_start: start of time window

window_end: end of time window

NIRV: near-infrared reflectance of vegetation (NIR × NDVI)

window_id: time window ID

match_count: number of valid observations in the window

NIRvP_PAR: NIRV × PAR

NIRvP_JAXAPAR: NIRV × JAXAPAR
