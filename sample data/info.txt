Sample Data Description

Column Definitions
date: Observation date.
GPP: Gross Primary Productivity.
SW_IN_F: Incoming shortwave radiation (W/m²).
PPFD_IN_F_MDS: Gap-filled photosynthetic photon flux density (µmol m^-2 s^-1).
PPFD_IN_F_MDS_QC: Quality control flag for PPFD data.
PAR_BK: Backup PAR estimated from shortwave radiation.
PAR: Photosynthetically active radiation used in analysis.
JAXAPAR: PAR derived from satellite data.
window_start: Start date of the compositing window.
window_end: End date of the compositing window.
NIRV: Near-infrared reflectance of vegetation.
window_id: Identifier of the compositing window.
match_count: Number of valid matched observations in the window.
NIRvP_PAR: NIRV multiplied by PAR.
NIRvP_JAXAPAR: NIRV multiplied by JAXAPAR.

Recommended Columns
For basic analysis:
date, GPP, NIRvP_PAR

For comparison between PAR sources:
date, GPP, NIRvP_PAR, NIRvP_JAXAPAR

For quality control:
PPFD_IN_F_MDS_QC, match_count

For radiation-related interpretation:
SW_IN_F, PAR, JAXAPAR, NIRV

Notes
NIRvP is calculated as NIRV × PAR or NIRV × JAXAPAR.
Recommended dependent variable: GPP.
Recommended explanatory variables: NIRvP_PAR and NIRvP_JAXAPAR.
